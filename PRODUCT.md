# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
People seeking a hardcore focus application to mercilessly block distractions and enforce productivity. They may struggle with digital distractions like games or social media and need system-level intervention to stay on task.

## Product Purpose
A marketing landing page for the Aurora app. It displays core features (System Blocker, Hardcore Mode, AI Evaluation) and provides dynamic download links to the latest alpha/beta binaries via GitHub.

## Brand Personality
Focused, calm, organized, unstoppable, hardcore. The brand feels somewhat dark and intense, yet minimalist and clean ("Light up your mind").

## Anti-references
- Playful or childish designs
- Busy, overwhelming layouts

## Design Principles
- **Uncompromising Focus**: The design should reflect the app's hardcore nature—no unnecessary distractions or clutter.
- **Calm Intensity**: Use the dark aesthetic and targeted neon glows to create a feeling of serious, "locked-in" energy.
- **Direct Utility**: Make the path to download and understanding the features immediate and obvious.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
No specific requirements beyond standard best practices (e.g., adequate contrast, semantic HTML). The site should respect user preferences like `prefers-reduced-motion` (already partially addressed via a "performance mode").
